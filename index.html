The Game

Where Strategy Meets the Trading Floor

Ex-Change! is a fast-paced card game where every decision shifts the market. With just 30 cards numbered 1 to 30, players compete to read the table, time their trades, and walk away with the highest profit.

Simple enough to learn in two minutes. Deep enough to keep you coming back for more. No dice. No luck of the draw. Just pure strategy, bluffing, and timing — all in a compact, elegant package inspired by the drama of the stock exchange.

The Bear

Play the long game. Hold your cards close, watch the market shift, and cash out when the spread is at its widest. Patience is your greatest asset.

The Bull

Seize the moment. Reinvest aggressively, build your hand, and dominate the market with bold, decisive plays. Fortune favors the fearless.

How to Play

Three Steps to Market Mastery

1

Open the Market

Card #15 sets the opening index. Each player receives a portfolio of cards. The remaining deck becomes the draw pile. The trading floor is open.

2

Make Your Move

Play a card to set a new Market Price. Then choose: Reinvest to draw a new card and keep your options open, or Cash Out to score points equal to the price difference.

3

Claim Victory

When the market closes, the player with the most Victory Points earns the title of Market Master. Read the table. Time your trades. Win.

| Old Price − New Price | = Victory Points

The Dilemma

Reinvest or Cash Out?

Every turn presents the same deceptively simple choice. Reinvest to keep your hand stocked with opportunities — but score nothing. Or cash out for immediate points — but lose a card from your portfolio forever. The catch? Every card you play changes the market for everyone at the table.

Reinvest

Draw a card. Score nothing. Stay in the game longer.

Cash Out

Score the spread. Lose a card. Shrink your options.
